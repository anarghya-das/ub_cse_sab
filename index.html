The Student Advisory Board has three main goals:

Raise Awareness

We strive to raise more awareness of SAB's involvement so that we can better help the student body.

Create Engagement

We strive to create more opportunties to engage with the undergraduates of our department.

Further Improvement

We strive to further improve current events SAB partakes in, such as Open House and Kids Day.
